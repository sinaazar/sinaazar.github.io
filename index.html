I am a M.Sc. student in Artificial Intelligence at Amirkabir University of Technology and a Data Scientist at Cafe Bazaar. I'm working on Group Activity Recognition as my M.Sc. thesis under supervision of Dr. Ahmad Nickabadi. Previously, I obtained my BSc in Software Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology where I worked on a face recognition software based on deep learning for my BSc thesis (2012-2016).

I enjoy analyzing various types of data. This data can be image, video, text, or the data of users of an application. Currently, my research is more concentrated on analyzing videos or images with humans in them. Probabilistic Graphical Models are interesting because of their abilities to solve complex and high level problems. I try to develop Deep Models that are able to tackle such problems like recognizing the collective activity of a group of persons. Also, it is of high importance for me to be able to come up with efficient deep architectures that can be used in various low-computational devices especially for processing videos.

My research interests are:

  • Fundamental Topics
    • Deep Learning
    • Computational Efficiency of Deep Models
    • Generative Adversarial Models
    • Probabilistic Graphical Models
  • Applications
    • Human Activity Recognition (specifically Group Activities) in image or video
    • Person Recognition in unconstrained images where face information may not be available
    • Human Pose Estimation in Video
    • Face Recognition
